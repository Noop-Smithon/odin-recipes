Chicken-Soup Recipe

The spicy chicke-soup is a soup like any other. With meticulous preparation you get a soup that can be dished for different occasions. As the name goes, we know it's predominantly going to be stocked with a lot of chicken and any other condiments desired. There's not a fits-it-all approach here, but I'll share my own routine.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 carrots, halved
  • 4 stalks celery, halved
  • 1 large onion
  • water to cover
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon granules (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the carrots, celery, onion and peper appropriately
  2. Put the chicken, carrots, celery and onion in a large soup pot and cover with cold water.
  3. Heat and simmer, uncovered, until the chicken meat falls off of the bones (skim off foam every so often). Don't forget to spice up your chicken at this stage and make sure to not overcook it too
  4. Take everything out of the pot and strain the broth.
  5. Pick the meat off of the bones and season the broth with salt, spices and chicken bouillon to taste
  6. Return the chicken, carrots, celery and onion to the pot, stir together, and serve.
